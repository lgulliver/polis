# Emergence modules

These modules are intentionally future-facing. They are not implemented in the first milestone, but the project should leave room for them.

## Survival

Resource needs, shelter, food pressure, injury, and risk management.

## Community

Groups, belonging, role specialization, norms, and internal coordination.

## Economy

Exchange, debt, inventories, production chains, and unequal access to useful goods.

## Governance

Rules, offices, legitimacy, punishment, voting, councils, or command structures.

## Religion

Rituals, sacred places, myths, taboos, and symbolic coordination.

## Conflict

Competition, betrayal, deterrence, raids, revenge, peacemaking, and ceasefires.

## Dialect

Shared shorthand, in-jokes, semantic drift, naming conventions, and subgroup speech styles.

## Scientists

Observation routines, hypothesis formation, experiments, record-keeping, and transmission of findings.
